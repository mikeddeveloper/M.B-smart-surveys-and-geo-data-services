— About the firm

Surveyors of record.
Partners in progress.

M.B Smart Surveys & Geo-Data Services is a Nigerian firm registered under the Corporate Affairs Commission in line with the Companies and Allied Matters Act, 1990. Our principal partners are Registered Surveyors and active Members of the Nigerian Institution of Surveyors.

Long before registration, our principals led projects as party leaders, team leaders and project managers. Today our field personnel carry more than five years of combined experience delivering integrated solutions to public and private clients.

Vision

To be recognized experts in Mapping Services, Cadastral and Layout Surveys — and trusted Expert Witnesses in the Court of Law.

Mission

To discharge our duties professionally, diligently and ethically — to the delight of every client we serve.

Values

We adhere to the rules and regulations set by the Nigerian Institution of Surveyors — and deliver full value for every engagement.
